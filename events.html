Workshop on Block Chain Technology

14 November 2019

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, Dehradun

UPES Application Day

Presents Workshop on Blockchain


An opportunity to learn, practice and create.

With that idea in mind, the UNITED CLUB OF DEVELOPERS (UCD) proudly presents you UPES Applications Day. An event not only unique in its name but also in practice. Every day, we drag ourselves through the same old hectic routine which ultimately leads to nowhere. And it’s no secret that in this era of competition, just maintaining a good attendance doesn’t necessarily helps a student in the long run. So here we are, UCD with the help of University of petroleum and energy studies is organizing a workshop on BLOCKCHAIN which will help students sharpen their skills in the ever-growing field of Application development. This event is sponsored by UPES itself and certificates will also be provided to all the aspirants taking part in this event. The event will be conducted by Mr. Yash Sharma who is a blockchain professional and a banking and payment solutions architect. The event is an open to all, as we are happy to provide the best for any curious geek out there.

Event details are as follows-:

Organized by-:
United Club of Developers (UCD) in association with University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES).

Aims and objectives-:
1. To help students gain information regarding latest technologies in the field of application development.
2. To make students familiar with design, concept and working of BLOCKCHAIN.
3. Interaction with the new members of club will also be a minor objective of this event.
4. To persuade and induce a sense of freedom of thoughts and ideas in the minds of students.
5. UCD believes in teamwork and collaborative learning. This event will also help students get familiar with basic UCD code of conduct.

Date of commemoration-: 14th November 2019
Venue-: Room no. 11002
Timing-: 10 AM onwards

“Divided by languages, united by code”
